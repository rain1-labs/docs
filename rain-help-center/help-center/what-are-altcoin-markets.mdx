---
title: "What are Altcoin Markets?"
---

While USDT on Arbitrum is the primary stablecoin for the platform, **Altcoin Markets** allow users to create, trade, and settle markets using other tokens natively on the Arbitrum network. We aim to eventually support using various tokens on multiple chains, so that **anyone could create a market using their own token**. This will also allow communities and DAOs to utilize their own native assets to power prediction markets.

**\$RAIN Markets**

Currently, the platform supports the **\$RAIN** token. This means the entire lifecycle of the market takes place using \$RAIN:

- **Market Creation:** You can launch a market funded entirely by \$RAIN (minimum liquidity requirement is equivalent to \$10).
- **Liquidity & Trading:** Liquidity providers add funds in \$RAIN, and traders buy and sell shares using \$RAIN.
- **Payouts:** All winnings and platform rewards are distributed in \$RAIN.

**Identifying Altcoin Markets**

You can easily spot these markets on the Explore page. Look for the **Altcoin** tab in the categories list or the **Trade with** badge on the market card, which indicates which currency is required to participate.
