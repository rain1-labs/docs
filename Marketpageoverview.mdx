---
title: "Market page overview"
---

The market page is where you find all the information for a specific prediction. This is where you can analyze the current odds, review recent activity, and place your trade.

Here’s a breakdown of the market page, which is split into two main columns.

### **Main Content (Left Column)**

This area shows all the core information and activity for the market, from top to bottom:

- **Market Details:** At the very top, you'll see the market's main **question**, the **Total Volume** (all money traded since it was created), the current **Pot Size** (total liquidity), and the **Ends In** timer, which shows when the market closes for predictions.
- **Probability Chart:** A graph that displays the price history for each outcome, showing you how sentiment has changed over time.
- **Outcomes:** This section shows all possible outcomes (e.g., "Yes," "No"). For each one, you can see:
  - **Chance:** The current probability (e.g., 82%) based on the funds in the pool.
  - **Volume:** The total amount of money placed on that specific outcome.
  - Clicking the down arrow on an outcome expands it to show more details about your position, including My Shares (how many shares you are currently holding), Option Ownership (the percentage of the total shares you are currently holding), Line (the payout multiplier), Potential PnL, Dynamic Payout, and the current Price Per Share.
- **Market Info:** This box gives you important context, including:
  - **Description:** Any extra details or rules provided by the market creator.
  - **Resolver:** Shows who is responsible for reporting the final outcome. This will be either the **Creator** or the **AI oracle** (Delphi).
- **Activity Tabs:** At the bottom, you can find more details about the market's activity:
  - **Comments:** A place to discuss the market with other participants.
  - **Activity:** A live feed of recent trades.
  - **Top Holders:** A list of the wallets that own the most shares for each outcome.
  - **Orders:** A log of your **Open Orders** (pending limit orders) and **Order History**.
- **Related Markets:** At the very bottom, you may see a list of other markets you might be interested in.

### **Trading Panel (Right Column)**

This panel is where you take action and manage your position.

- **Trading Interface:** This is the main action box where you can Buy or Sell options. Learn more about [<u>trading options</u>](tradingoptions).
- **My Position:** Here you can see your personal stake in the market, including your total **Invested Amount** and the number of **shares** you hold for each outcome.
- **Order Book:** Lists all active **Limit** orders that are waiting to be matched.

**Liquidity:** This is where you can **Add Liquidity** to the market. Liquidity providers earn 1.2% of the fees from the market's trading volume.
