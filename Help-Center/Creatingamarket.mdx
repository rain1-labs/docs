---
title: "Creating a market"
---

You can create a new prediction market on Rain by following a few simple steps. You will need to define your market's topic, set its parameters, and provide the initial liquidity. Any user can create a market, and you'll have the option to make it either public or private.

Here’s how to create a market:

 1. Login to Rain.
 2. Select **Create** at the top right corner of the screen.

    ![Create Pn](/images/create.png)
 3. A pop-up will appear, asking you to choose between a [**<u>Public</u>**](publicmarkets) or [**<u>Private</u>**](privatemarkets) market. A public market can be accessed by anyone, and its outcome can be resolved by the creator or automatically by an AI oracle. A private market can only be accessed with a code and is resolved by the market's creator (which means you will be the one to resolve the market if you choose to create a private market).
 4. Choose the type of market you want to create, and then fill in the required information. You'll need to write your question and at least two possible options for the outcome. \
    **Note:** you can add up to 26 options for the outcome.
 5. **Optional:** You can also add a description and up to 18 tags to help others find your market.
 6. Set the **Start Date** and **End Date** for your market. This defines the time frame during which participants can make their predictions.
 7. Click **Continue**. On the **Add Liquidity** screen, you will need to add initial liquidity for your market. A minimum of \$10 is required. You can also adjust how this liquidity is distributed among the different outcome options by using the sliders. Click **Continue** to advance to the next step.
 8. Choose the market resolution method: you can choose between **Manual Resolution** and automatic **AI-Powered Resolution**. If you choose to have the market resolved by the AI oracle, you'll be charged an additional fee of \$1.
 9. Click **Continue**.
10. The final screen will show a **Confirmation** of all the details you've entered. Review your information carefully.

    <img
      src="/images/marketcreateconfirmation.png"
      alt="Marketcreateconfirmation Pn"
      title="Marketcreateconfirmation Pn"
      style={{ width:"53%" }}
    />
11. Select **Confirm** to complete the process. A transaction will be initiated to create your market, and a confirmation will appear once it is processed successfully.\
    **Note:** you may need to sign the transaction in your wallet again, so make sure to check it for any signature requests.
12. Now that you have confirmed the details, the market you created will go live on the start date and time you set.
13. If your market is public, it will be listed on the Rain app's homepage for anyone to join. If you created a private market, you will need to share the link and access code with other users you want to invite so they can participate.
