**Before you begin:**

To get started on the Rain platform, you'll need to connect your wallet. Rain supports cross-chain deposits, allowing you to add funds from various popular blockchain networks and use multiple tokens.

1. **Supported Blockchain Networks:** Rain is built natively on Arbitrum, but also enables participation from Ethereum, BNB Chain, and Base.
2. **Supported Deposit Tokens:** You can deposit USDT, USDC, Ethereum, BNB, and Base on the applicable networks.

Rain handles the bridging and conversion for cross-chain deposits, simplifying the process for participants from different ecosystems to join and trade without technical hurdles.
