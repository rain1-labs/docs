---
title: "Connect your wallet"
---

1. In your browser, go to https://www.rain.one/.
2. In the top right corner of the page, select **Login**.
3. A pop-up will appear with wallet connection options. Choose your preferred option from the list, such as Metamask or WalletConnect.
4. Your wallet will prompt you to connect to the Rain platform. Confirm the connection.
5. After connecting, a signature request will appear. You will need to sign this to proceed.
6. Once you sign the request, you'll be logged in to the app, and your in-app balance will be displayed in the top right corner.

Upon completing the wallet connection process, a new user account is created for you within the system, along with a unique internal wallet that will be used for trading on the platform.
