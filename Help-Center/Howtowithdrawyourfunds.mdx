You can withdraw your funds from the Rain platform to any external wallet address you choose. Since Rain's prediction markets operate on the **Arbitrum** network, all withdrawals are processed in **USDT** on the Arbitrum network.

Follow the steps below to withdraw funds from Rain:

1. Click on the dropdown menu in the top-right corner, where your balance is displayed, and select **My Profile**.
2. On your profile page, click the **Withdraw** button under **Portfolio**.
3. In the pop-up that opens, enter the **Amount** of USDT you wish to withdraw, or click **MAX** to withdraw your entire available balance.
4. Enter the **Address** where you want to receive the funds. This must be a wallet address that supports receiving USDT on the Arbitrum network.


1. To finalize the transaction, click **Withdraw**.
2. You may be prompted to sign and confirm the transaction in your connected wallet.

Once the transaction is validated on-chain, the USDT will be transferred to the external wallet address you provided.

\
_Note: As with most decentralized transactions, a small amount of _**_Gas Fees_**_ (paid in USDT on Arbitrum) is required to process the withdrawal. This fee will be deducted from the amount being withdrawn._
