---
title: "Inflation/deflation mechanism"
---

The **\$RAIN** token has an initial total supply of 1.15 trillion tokens. The protocol includes both inflationary and deflationary mechanisms to balance ecosystem growth and token scarcity.

- **Deflationary Mechanism**: A portion of the platform fees is used to buy back and burn \$RAIN tokens. Specifically, 2.5% of the trading volume from every prediction market is used for this purpose. This creates constant downward pressure on the supply as platform usage increases, directly linking the token's scarcity to protocol activity and adoption.
- **Inflationary Mechanism**: To ensure sustainable ecosystem growth, Rain utilizes a dynamic inflation model where an amount equal to 10% of the tokens burned through the Buyback and Burn mechanism is automatically minted. These tokens are manually claimed by the Rain team and allocated to the Foundation to support key initiatives, including team rewards, ecosystem development, marketing campaigns, strategic partnerships, and community contributors, creating a balanced economy that aligns deflationary pressure with incentive-driven growth.
