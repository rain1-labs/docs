The **\$RAIN** token has an initial total supply of 1.15 trillion tokens. The protocol includes both inflationary and deflationary mechanisms to balance ecosystem growth and token scarcity.

- **Deflationary Mechanism**: A portion of the platform fees is used to buy back and burn \$RAIN tokens. Specifically, 2.5% of the trading volume from every prediction market is used for this purpose. This creates constant downward pressure on the supply as platform usage increases, directly linking the token's scarcity to protocol activity and adoption.
- **Inflationary Mechanism**: To support ongoing growth and incentivize contributors, a controlled inflation model is in place. The protocol will begin minting \$50,000 worth of RAIN tokens daily starting in month four after launch. In months 4-12, these newly minted tokens are allocated to the DAO. Later on, they will be used to support team rewards, ecosystem development, marketing campaigns, and strategic partnerships.
