---
title: "Cross-chain support"
---

Rain supports cross-chain deposits, allowing users to add funds from popular blockchain networks and use multiple tokens. The core supported network for protocol operations is Arbitrum, but Rain also enables participation from Ethereum, BNB Chain, and Base, with stablecoins like USDT and USDC used for market deposits and the RAIN token for governance and rewards.​

**Supported Blockchain Networks**

- **Arbitrum:** Rain is built natively on Arbitrum, which powers its prediction markets, automated liquidity, and seamless user experience.​
- **Ethereum:** While Arbitrum is primary, users can connect wallets from Ethereum and participate in markets, with funds bridged for cross-chain compatibility as Rain expands cross-chain support.​
- **BNB Chain** (Binance Smart Chain): Rain’s infrastructure allows users on BNB Chain to connect and deposit supported assets for use in prediction markets.​
- **Base:** The Rain protocol is extending support for Base, enabling deposits from users on Base to participate in Rain’s ecosystem.​
- **Bitcoin:** You can deposit BTC and have it converted automatically to USDT on Arbitrum for use on the platform.
