---
title: "Public markets"
---

Public markets are open to anyone who wants to participate. You can see available public markets by clicking **Explore Markets** on Rain's [<u>homepage</u>](https://app.rain.one/), and anyone can click to join them from there. These markets are ideal for broader topics like global predictions or sports.

**Who can create one?** 

Any user can create a public market.

**How are they resolved?** 

When creating a public market, you can choose to resolve the market yourself or have it resolved automatically by Delphi, an advanced AI-based oracle developed by [<u>Olympus AI</u>](https://olympusai.io/). The AI oracle is designed to provide objective and unbiased resolution for publicly verifiable events, ensuring that outcomes are determined accurately and without manipulation. Delphi uses a consensus-driven system to verify information, with an answer confirmed only when at least three of its five "Explorer Agents" agree. The oracle is designed to always select one of the listed options to ensure the market is resolved. 

**Note:** An extra \$1 fee is collected in public markets if the AI oracle is chosen as the resolver.

**What if there's a dispute?** 

After Delphi's initial resolution, participants have a set time frame to file a dispute if they disagree. To file a dispute, you must put up collateral. If a dispute is filed, the case is escalated to a group of decentralized human oracles for a final decision. If the human oracles determine that the disputing party was correct, that party gets their collateral back. If the human oracles decide that Delphi was right, the collateral goes to Delphi.
