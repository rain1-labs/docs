A market is a place on Rain where you can make a prediction by placing funds on the outcome of an event. Rain allows users to create markets for a wide range of events. The funds within a market are automatically distributed among the possible outcomes, with the price of each option dynamically reflecting the proportion of the total funds placed on it.

Rain offers two types of markets to meet different user needs: [<u>public </u>](Help-Center/Publicmarkets.mdx)and [<u>private</u>](Help-Center/Privatemarkets.mdx).
