The **\$RAIN** token is the native governance token for the Rain protocol, currently operating on Arbitrum. It's designed to be central to the protocol's evolution, combining both inflationary and deflationary mechanisms to create a sustainable and incentive-aligned token economy. While not required for using the app, holding the token allows you to participate in the protocol's governance and shape its future.

For more information and full tokenomics, [<u>read our whitepaper</u>](https://whitepaper.rain.one/).
