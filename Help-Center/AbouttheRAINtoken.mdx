---
title: "About the $RAIN token"
---

\$RAIN is the native token of the Rain protocol, operating on the Arbitrum network. Holding the \$RAIN token is required to access Trading Power and participate in the platform's prediction markets.

Token holders exercise their influence on the protocol’s development through the Rain DAO, the decentralized autonomous organization responsible for all governance decisions.

The protocol rewards key contributors - market creators, liquidity providers, and resolvers - directly in \$RAIN tokens. This system aligns incentives and encourages active participation in the ecosystem.

A deflationary buyback and burn mechanism is also a core feature. From every market's trading volume, 2.5% is used to buy back and burn \$RAIN tokens, directly linking the token's scarcity to platform activity.

For more information and full tokenomics, [<u>read our whitepaper</u>](https://whitepaper.rain.one/).
