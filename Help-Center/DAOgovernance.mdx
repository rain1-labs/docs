---
title: "DAO governance"
---

At launch, the primary utility of **\$RAIN** is governance. This means token holders can participate in the platform's decentralized governance by submitting or voting on proposals to shape the protocol's future. You do not need to hold \$RAIN to use the Rain app or participate in markets. The DAO may also decide on additional token utilities later on, such as requiring a minimum token holding to create a market.
