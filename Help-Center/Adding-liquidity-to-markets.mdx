---
title: "Adding Liquidity To Markets"
---

Liquidity is the fuel that keeps Rain markets running smoothly. Because Rain uses an Automated Market Maker (AMM) model - similar to a decentralized exchange like Uniswap - users trade against a liquidity pool rather than directly against other users.

By adding liquidity, you help ensure there is always enough capital in the pool for others to buy and sell options instantly. In return for providing this service, you are rewarded based on the activity in that market.

### **Why add liquidity?**

When you add liquidity to a market, you become a **Liquidity Provider (LP)**. You aren't taking a specific side (like betting "Yes" or "No"). Instead, you are funding the entire market.

- **Earn from Volume:** Liquidity providers collectively earn **1.2% of the total trading volume** generated by that market. As the market sees more activity and volume increases, your rewards grow.
- **Support the Ecosystem:** Deep liquidity stabilizes prices and allows for larger trades without significant price impact (slippage), making the market more attractive to traders.

### **How to add liquidity**

You can add liquidity to any active market, whether you created it or not.

**Note:** You must add liquidity in the specific currency that the market is denominated in. Currently, markets on Rain are funded with either **USDT** or **\$RAIN**.

1. Navigate to the specific market page you wish to support.
2. On the right-hand side of the screen, scroll down to the **Liquidity** panel.
3. Click **Add Liquidity**.
4. In the pop-up window, enter the **Amount** you wish to deposit.
5. Click **Continue**.
6. Confirm the transaction in your wallet.

Once the transaction is confirmed, your funds are added to the pool, and you will begin accruing rewards based on the market's trading volume.

You can view your current standing in the **Liquidity** panel on the market page, under **My Liquidity Position**.
