---
title: "Closing a market"
---

After a market's scheduled end time has arrived, anyone who has access to the market can manually close it. This action locks the market, preventing new predictions from being placed. Trading of existing options, however, can continue on the secondary market until the winner is decided

**How to close the market**

1. On the market's page, look for the message **"market has ended. Close market to decide the winner"** on the right side of the screen, and click the **Close market** button below it.
2. In the confirmation pop-up, you'll see a reminder that this action is irreversible. To proceed, click **YES I'M SURE**.

Once the market is closed, the winning option can be decided to resolve the market.

**Deciding the winner**

- **For private markets:** The market creator is the resolver and decides the winner. After closing the market, click the **Decide Winner** button under the message \*\*"market is closed!” \*\*in the top right of the page. In the pop-up that appears, select the winning option and then click **Continue**.
- **For public markets:** The winner is decided by Delphi, an AI oracle developed by Olympus AI. After the market is closed. Delphi will automatically decide the winner.

Once the winner has been decided, it's no longer possible to place new predictions or trade existing options.

**The dispute window**

Once the winner has been decided, a dispute window of 15 minutes opens, during which participants can file a dispute if they disagree with the outcome. If no dispute is created within the set timeframe, the resolution becomes final and participants can claim their rewards. [<u>Learn how to open a dispute</u>](FilingaDispute).
