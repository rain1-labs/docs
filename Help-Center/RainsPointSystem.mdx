---
title: "The Raindrops System"
---

Raindrops is our lightweight points system exclusively for users of our beta version.

While we prepare for the official rewards program - complete with structured seasons, ranks, and token airdrops - Raindrops is a temporary system designed to reward early activity on the platform. 

**Earning Raindrops:** You can earn Raindrops by depositing funds, trading, adding liquidity, and inviting others.

![Actions&rain Raindrops Pn](/images/Actions&RAINRaindrops.png)

**Purpose of Raindrops:** Raindrops do not yet carry a monetary value but may influence early airdrops or seasonal status later on.

**Allocation:** 2% (23 billion tokens) of the Ecosystem Growth & Staking allocation is designated for the Raindrops system, released via a 12-month linear vesting schedule. Further details regarding the system's structure and conversion plan will be published later.
