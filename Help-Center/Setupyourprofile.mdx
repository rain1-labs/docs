You can add certain details to personalize your profile, like a profile image, your name, and links to your social accounts. When you add links to your Twitter, Discord, and Telegram accounts, clickable icons will appear on your profile.

**To edit your profile:**

1. From the Rain app homepage, click on the dropdown menu in the top-right corner (to the right of the **Create **button).
2. Select **My Profile**.

   <img
     src="/images/myprofile.png"
     alt="Myprofile Pn"
     title="Myprofile Pn"
     style={{ width:"41%" }}
   />
3. On your profile page, click **Edit Profile** in the top right.
4. Here, you can fill in your name and link your Twitter, Telegram, and Discord accounts.
5. To change your profile image, click **Change** or **Remove** next to the profile image icon. You can then upload a new image from your computer. The recommended image dimensions are 400x400 pixels, and the file size should be a maximum of 5MB in either JPEG or PNG format.
6. Once you're done, click **Save**.
