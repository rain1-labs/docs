---
title: "Referral Program"
---

Rain has a referral program that allows you to earn [<u>points</u>](https://docs.google.com/document/d/1k-DqWpDnkrniRQwxRgpDc7Nw41Kn1BGnLSByE_A_h8E/edit?pli=1&tab=t.0#heading=h.ri7h372c65n3) by inviting new users to the platform.

1. From your profile page, locate the **Invite & Earn** section.
2. Click on **Copy Referral Link**.
3. Share your unique link with friends.
4. When someone joins Rain using your link and signs up, both you and your friend will receive 1,000 [<u>Rain points</u>](https://docs.google.com/document/d/1k-DqWpDnkrniRQwxRgpDc7Nw41Kn1BGnLSByE_A_h8E/edit?pli=1&tab=t.0#heading=h.ri7h372c65n3).
