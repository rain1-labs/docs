---
title: "Referral Program"
---

Rain has a referral program that allows you to earn [<u>Raindrops</u>](RainsPointSystem) by inviting new users to the platform.

1. From your profile page, locate the **Invite & Earn** section.
2. Click on **Copy Referral Link**.
3. Share your unique link with friends.
4. When someone joins Rain using your link, signs up, and deposits a minimum of \$5, both you and your friend will receive 1,000 [<u>Raindrops</u>](RainsPointSystem).
