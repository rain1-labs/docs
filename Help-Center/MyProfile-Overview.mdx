---
title: "My Profile - Overview"
---

Your profile page provides a summary of your activity on the Rain platform, including your portfolio (your current in-app balance), the total number of predictions you participated in, your total PnL and past markets you created. From here, you can also manage your personal information.

**To get to your profile page:**

1. Log into your account (connect your wallet).
2. In the top right corner of the page, click on the dropdown menu (to the right of the Create button).
3. Select **My Profile** from the dropdown menu.

The profile page contains several sections:

\*\*User details: \*\*Your display name, profile image, social links, internal Smart Wallet and total predictions. Learn how to [<u>setup your profile</u>](https://docs.google.com/document/d/1k-DqWpDnkrniRQwxRgpDc7Nw41Kn1BGnLSByE_A_h8E/edit?pli=1&tab=t.0#heading=h.ptnbg0kuaqdm). 

\*\*Portfolio: \*\*View your current balance and use the buttons to [<u>Deposit</u>](https://docs.google.com/document/d/1k-DqWpDnkrniRQwxRgpDc7Nw41Kn1BGnLSByE_A_h8E/edit?pli=1&tab=t.0#heading=h.d85l8kt2m4i2) and Withdraw funds. 

\*\*Profit/Loss: \*\*This section tracks your trading and prediction outcomes over time. 

At the bottom of the profile page, you can review your past activities through several tabs. These include:

- \*\*My Predictions: \*\*Displays a list of all prediction markets you have participated in.
- \*\*Public markets: \*\*Shows only the public markets you have created.
- \*\*Private markets: \*\*Shows only the private markets you have created.

**History:** A log of your past deposits and withdrawals.
