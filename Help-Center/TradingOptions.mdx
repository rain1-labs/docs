Trading options on Rain allows you to buy or sell shares that represent a specific outcome in a market. Your shares represent your belief about the future outcome of an event, with the price dynamically adjusting based on the market's collective assessment. All funds are committed once you place a trade, and there is no refund mechanism.

When you buy an option, you are purchasing shares for a specific outcome.

When you sell an option, you are placing a conditional sell order for shares you currently own. This allows you to exit your position before the market closes. Your sell order is placed on an order book and matched with new buyers, which helps preserve market liquidity.

Rain offers two types of orders to trade options: 

1. **Market Orders**

A Market order allows you to place a trade immediately at the best available current market price. This is the fastest way to buy or sell sharesת as it does not wait for a specific price point.

**To place a Market order:**

1. On a market's page, click **Buy** or **Sell** on the right-hand panel.
2. Below the Buy button, Select **Market**.
3. Select the outcome you wish to trade.
4. Enter the amount of USDT you want to spend (for a buy order) or the amount of shares you want to sell (for a sell order).
5. Rain will display the estimated shares you will receive or the estimated USDT you will get.
6. Click **Place Trade** to submit your order.

**2. Limit Orders**

A Limit order allows you to place a conditional trade at a specific price you choose. This order will only be executed if the market price reaches your set price.

**To place a Limit order:**

1. On a market's page, click **Buy** or **Sell** on the right-hand panel.
2. Below the Sell button, select **Limit**.
3. Select the outcome you wish to trade.
4. Enter the amount of shares you want to trade and the desired price per share.
5. Click **Place Trade** to submit your order.

Your Limit order will then appear in the **Order Book** in the right-hand panel (along with any other limit orders), where it will wait to be matched with a buyer or seller. It will also appear under **Orders** \> **Open Orders** at the bottom of the page.
