title: "How to Claim Your Rewards"
description: "Description of your new file."
---

After a market has ended, the winner has been decided, and the dispute window has closed (or a dispute has been resolved), you can claim any rewards you may have won. 

**Note:** Before you can claim your rewards, you must first cancel any open orders you may have in that market.

1. On the market's page, scroll down to the Orders section at the bottom of the page.
2. Select the **Open Orders** tab and look for any open buy or sell orders.
3. If you have any open orders, click the\
    **Cancel** button next to each one.
4. Once all open orders have been cancelled, continue to claim your rewards.

Here's how to claim your rewards:

5. On the market's page, look for the **Claim Rewards** section in the right-hand panel.
6. Click the **Claim** button next to your winning shares. If you don’t see a Claim button, it means that you didn’t buy any shares of the winning option.
7. A pop-up will appear showing the transaction's progress (note that you may need to sign the transaction in your wallet). After a few moments, a success message will confirm that your reward has been transferred to your balance.
