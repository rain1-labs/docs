---

Private markets are designed for specialized or community-specific events and are not open to the public. You need a secret access code to participate in a private market.

Private markets are designed for more specific or community-based events and are not open to the public. To participate, you need a secret access code from the creator.

**Who can create one?** 

Any user can create a private market.

**How are they resolved?** 

The market creator acts as the resolver for private events. This allows for predictions that are specific to a certain context or group.

**What if there's a dispute?** 

It's important to trust the creator of a private event, as they have the sole authority to resolve the outcome and could potentially take advantage of participants. However, participants can dispute the outcome decided by the creator and escalate the matter to human oracles. If the disputing party wins, they get their collateral back. If the original resolver (the market creator) wins, the staked collateral goes to them.
