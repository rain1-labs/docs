title: "How to End Predictions in a market"
description: "Description of your new file."
---

As a market creator, you have the option to close a prediction market before its scheduled end time. This action is irreversible and stops all participants from making new predictions. You might want to use this feature if the event is canceled, the outcome becomes unexpectedly obvious, or if you realize you made a mistake when setting up the market.

To close a market, follow these steps:

1. On your market's page, click the **NO MORE PREDS** button on the right-hand panel.
2. A pop-up will appear, asking you to confirm your decision.\
   **Important:** Stopping the predictions is an irreversible action.
3. To proceed, click **YES I'M SURE**.

The market is now closed, and you can decide the winner, if applicable.
