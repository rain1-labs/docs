title: "Deposit Funds"
---

After connecting your wallet, you'll need to deposit funds to participate in prediction markets. 

Rain is built to support [**cross-chain deposits**](Cross-chainsupport), which means you can add funds using multiple tokens from various popular blockchain networks.

Think of this as a seamless **DeFi bridge**. While Rain's markets and core operations run natively on **Arbitrum**, you can also deposit assets from **Ethereum, BNB Chain, Base and Bitcoin**.

The platform simplifies this process significantly:

- **Supported Deposit Tokens:** You can deposit **USDT**, **USDC**, **Ethereum**, **BNB**, **Base **and **BTC** on the applicable networks.
- **Automatic Handling:** Rain takes care of the necessary **bridging and conversion** for cross-chain deposits.

**Initiating Your Deposit**

Select the dropdown menu in the top-right corner (to the right of the **Create** button), and then select **Deposit**. A window will appear, presenting two funding options:

**Option 1: Via Connected Wallet (Direct Deposit)**

This option currently supports only USDT on Arbitrum. If you don't already hold **USDT on Arbitrum** in your connected wallet, use option 2 below.

To deposit USDT on Arbitrum through this option:

1. Enter the **amount** of USDT you want to deposit.
2. Click **Deposit** and confirm the transaction in your connected wallet.

**Option 2: Via Deposit Address (Cross-Chain Swap)**

Use this if you are sending funds from an external address or utilizing our cross-chain functionality (e.g., sending ETH from the Ethereum network).

1. Choose the originating **Chain** (Arbitrum, Base, BSC/BNB Chain, Ethereum or Bitcoin) and the deposit **Token** (USDT, USDC, ETH, BNB or BTC) from the dropdowns.
2. Click **Generate Swap Request**.
3. A warning will appear, notifying that the generated deposit address is valid for **one swap only** and will remain valid for 2 minutes. Click **Okay** to continue.
4. A unique **deposit address** and corresponding **QR code** will be generated.
5. Send the exact amount of your chosen token from your external wallet to the provided deposit address. Rain will process the cross-chain transfer and credit your account with USDT on Arbitrum.

**_Important: Always ensure the token and chain you select in the app match the asset you are sending to prevent any delays or loss of funds._**
