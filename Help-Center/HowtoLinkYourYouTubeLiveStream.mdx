You can bring your YouTube Live streams directly to a prediction market page to engage with your audience. This feature allows you to embed your live video for all participants to watch while they are making their predictions.

1. On a prediction market page, scroll down to find the **Live Stream** section on the right side of the screen.
2. Copy the URL of your YouTube Live stream.
3. Paste the URL into the field labeled "**Enter Link to your YouTube Live stream**".
4. Click the **Start** button.

Your YouTube Live stream will now be displayed on the prediction page. You can stop streaming anytime by clicking the STOP button in the Live Stream section on the right.

**Examples of how you might use this feature:**

- A user who creates a market about the outcome of a soccer match could live stream the game itself.
- You could live stream a company's internal table tennis match for which a private market has been created.
- A gamer could stream a live tournament, where participants are predicting the winner.
- A community member could stream a press conference or political debate for a market about a specific outcome.
