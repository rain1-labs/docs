---
title: "Filing a Dispute"
---

If you believe a market's outcome has been decided incorrectly, you can file a dispute to have the decision reviewed by human oracles provided by Olympus AI. The dispute must be opened within the 15-minute dispute window that opens after the winner is announced. To file a dispute, you must put up a collateral of 0.1% of the market volume or \$1,000, whichever is less.

To file a dispute:

1. On the market's page, after the winner has been decided, click the **CREATE A DISPUTE** button.
2. A confirmation pop-up will appear, showing you the calculated fee for opening the dispute and the estimated gas fee.
3. Click **YES, CREATE DISPUTE** to proceed.

After a dispute is filed, the resolution is escalated to decentralized human oracles, who will determine the correct outcome. The resolution process can take up to two hours. The collateral will be distributed to the winning party, while the human oracles who adjudicated the dispute will receive the resolver's reward (0.1% of the market’s volume).
