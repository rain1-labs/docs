The **Raindrops** section provides a comprehensive overview of the programs designed to recognize and reward your activity and contributions to the Rain ecosystem. You can access this page by clicking the dropdown menu in the top-right corner (next to the Create button) and selecting Rewards.

This page is broken down into three key areas:

- **Invite & Earn** (Referral Program): Find your unique referral link here. You can copy this link and share it with friends. When a friend joins using your link, both of you can receive 1,000 Raindrops. Learn more about [<u>Rain’s referral Program</u>](ReferralProgram).
- **Get Quick Raindrops**: Earn Raindrops for completing a simple, one-time task - following [Rain on X](https://x.com/Rain__Protocol).
- **Raindrops**: Earn Raindrops by depositing funds, trading, adding liquidity, and inviting friends. Learn more about the [<u>Raindrops</u>](RainsPointSystem) system.

**Raindrops Breakdown:** The bottom of the page features a detailed table showing how you have accumulated Raindrops over time.
