The Entropy Layer uses a simplified fee model to support high-velocity, randomly pre-generated scenarios and shared liquidity. 

For every market created on this layer, a **4% fee** is deducted from the total trading volume. This fee is split into two parts:

- **\$RAIN Buyback-and-Burn (2.5%)**: This portion is used to purchase and burn \$RAIN tokens, linking the activity on the Entropy Layer to the protocol’s token scarcity.
- **Liquidity Pool Incentives (1.5%)**: These funds are directed to the limited shared liquidity pool to reward providers for supporting the layer's instant settlement and high-frequency action.

### Builder Rewards

As with the standard protocol, builders using the SDK to launch applications on the Entropy Layer are rewarded for the activity they generate.

- **Baseline Reward**: You receive **0.5%** of the total trading volume generated by your application.
