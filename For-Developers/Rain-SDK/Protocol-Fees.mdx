uilding on Rain Protocol means plugging into a sustainable ecosystem where every participant is incentivized to keep the markets moving. The protocol uses a transparent fee structure to reward the people providing the logic, liquidity, and resolutions.

### **Standard Trading Fees**

Every market on Rain collected a **5% fee** from its total trading volume. This fee is automatically split to support the protocol and those who help maintain it:

- **Market Creator (1%)**: For the user or builder who launched the market.
- **Liquidity Providers (1.2%)**: For those funding the pool to enable trading.
- **Resolver (0.3%)**: For the party that verifies and settles the final outcome.
- **\$RAIN Buyback-and-Burn (2.5%)**: Half of every fee is used to buy and burn \$RAIN tokens, directly linking token scarcity to protocol usage.
- **AI Oracle Fee**: Public markets using the AI Oracle for resolution incur an additional **\$1 flat fee** from the creator to cover the oracle's services.

**Note on Resolution Rewards:** If a market uses the AI Oracle, the 0.3% is split between the Oracle (0.1%) and the user who triggers the market closure (0.2%). In manual resolutions, the closer receives the full 0.3% if their proposed answer is correct.

### **Builder Rewards**

Our Builder Program is designed for those launching their own frontends or niche applications using the Rain SDK. As a builder, you earn a direct share of the activity your platform generates.

- **Baseline Reward**: Builders receive a percentage of the total trading volume generated by their application. The exact percentage will be finalized soon through a DAO decision..
- **Stacking Rewards**: Builders can significantly increase these earnings by taking on multiple roles. If you act as the market creator, initial liquidity provider, and resolver, your total rewards can reach up to **2.5%** of the volume.
